## [VScode配置latex教程](https://blog.csdn.net/qq_44089921/article/details/107719981) [latex语法教程](https://blog.csdn.net/tianzong2019/article/details/106521432)

1. [texlive下载链接](https://tug.org/texlive/acquire-iso.html)  
    - 建议以**管理员**身份安装，勾选**取消安装texworks前端**。  
    - `xelatex -v`查看是否安装成功
2. 安装vscode插件 `latex workshop`
3. VScode settings.json
```json

    "latex-workshop.latex.autoBuild.run": "never",
    "latex-workshop.showContextMenu": true,
```
